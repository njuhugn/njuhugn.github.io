I got PhD in CSE at HKUST in 2021 and was an HKPFS (2016-2020). I got both bachelor (2013) and master (2016) degrees from Nanjing University (see homepage @NLPLab). My research interests are recommender systems, transfer learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and privacy-preserving. I have over ten publications appeared in top conferences including WWW, EMNLP, NAACL, CIKM, and IJCAI, and in top journals including TKDD. Moreover, I have served as reviewer for top journals (incl. IEEE TPAMI) and the PC member for top conferences (incl. ACL/EMNLP/NAACL/EACL, ICML/NeurIPS/ICLR, AAAI/IJCAI, CSCW, ICWSM) over 30 times.
