Dataset & Benchmark

  1. INSCIT: Information-Seeking Conversations with Mixed-Initiative Interactions. Wu et al. arXiv, 2022 [GitHub repo]
    dataset for information-seeking conversations with mixed-initiative interactions
  2. doc2dial: A Goal-Oriented Document-Grounded Dialogue Dataset. Feng et al. EMNLP, 2020 [dataset]
    goal-oriented dialogues in 4 social-welfare domains, with an additional Covid19 domain in the blind held-out test set
  3. Wizard of Wikipedia: Knowledge-Powered Conversational Agents. Dinan et al. ICLR, 2029 [dataset]
    Wizard of Wikipedia (WoW), an open-domain dialogue task for training agents that can converse knowledgably about open-domain topics
  4. MultiWOZ - A Large-Scale Multi-Domain Wizard-of-Oz Dataset for Task-Oriented Dialogue Modelling. Budzianowski et al. EMNLP, 2018 [GitHub repo]
    Multi-Domain Wizard-of-Oz dataset (MultiWOZ)
