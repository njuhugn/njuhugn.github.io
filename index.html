Guangneng Hu
I got PhD in CSE at HK Univ. of Sci. & Tech. in 2021 and received the HKPFS (2016-2020). I got both bachelor (2013) and master (2016) degrees from Nanjing University. My research interests are deep transfer learning, natural language processing, recommender systems, and privacy-preserving. I have eight first-author research publications appeared in top conferences including WWW, EMNLP, NAACL, CIKM, and IJCAI, and in top journals including TKDD. Moreover, I have served as reviewer & PC member for top journals (incl. IEEE TPAMI) & conferences (incl. ACL/EMNLP/NAACL, ICML/NeurIPS/ICLR, AAAI/IJCAI, CSCW/ICWSM) over 30 times.
